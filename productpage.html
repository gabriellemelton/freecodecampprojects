Recon Trooper Skin



Sporting a tank top, cargo pants, combat boots, gloves, and a fishing cap, the Recon Trooper Skin is considered to be the rarest skin in the game. It was released on October 30th, 2017 and has only made four appearances in the store since that time. It costs 1200 VBucks, which equates to $12 USD.
